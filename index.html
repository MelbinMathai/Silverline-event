Expert Doctors Unite at Silverline's 2023 Scientific Meeting

Silverline, a boutique hospital in Cochin, has always prioritized the well-being of our patients. In addition to providing exceptional care for individuals with diabetes, thyroid disorders, and other endocrine-related diseases, we raise awareness about these conditions among the public. We believe that together, we are fighting against these serious health conditions, and strive to educate people in every way we can.

Now, we are pleased to announce a scientific meeting on November 5th, 2023, at Holiday Inn Kochi, where we will bring together a group of expert doctors. By attending, you contribute to the collective effort of fighting these diseases and promoting better health for all.
